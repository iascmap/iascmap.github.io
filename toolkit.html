Help us to build a partnership toolkit!

The Slavery-Free Communities team at the University of Nottingham is building a new Anti-Slavery Partnership toolkit to assist communities and service providers in working together to improve responses to modern slavery.

This resource will help with:

  • Setting an agenda and developing a work-plan
  • Taking joint actions & connecting with other partnerships
  • Securing resources
  • Establishing partnership governance and accountability frameworks
  • Measuring and monitoring performance
  • Engaging with survivors
  • Increasing public awareness, community mobilisation and co-production

Send us your ideas

This project is a collaborative effort, we would appreciate information and examples relating to any of the above points.

For instance, case studies of initiatives that have worked well, examples of action plans, information-sharing agreements, modern slavery statements and monitoring frameworks.
